## Booking Rules

- Appointment duration is in minutes.
- Staff may have multiple roles.
- Staff may have multiple skills.
- One room cannot host two appointments simultaneously.
- Devices may require cooldown time.
- Booking requests from Telegram require secretary approval.
- Phone bookings are supported.
- Deposit payment will be supported.
